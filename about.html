About Us

We are a community driven marketplace that was created by Zanothando sithole to empower communities by providing them with a platform to sell their products and earn an income.

The business has been operational since 2025. The business supports and encourages entrepreneurship. We are passionate about our customers, sellers and our business.

Mission

Our mission is to create a community driven marketplace where local vendors and entrepreneurs are given a platform to promote and sell their products. We aim to provide ease of access to high quality products to all community members, this creates collaboration, innovation and community engagement.

Vision

Our vision is to become a reliable marketplace that fosters strong community connections and encourages sustainable entrepreneurship. We aim to grow the business from being a physical place to an online marketplace where local products can be valued more and create visibility for both business and its vendors.
